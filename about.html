Welcome to Next Models,
a model agency based in Hamburg/Germany.
Since 2014, we have been discovering talent for fashion and advertising.

Our mission is to empower new faces and tell real stories through fashion. We don’t chase trends — we build careers.
With personal coaching, professional guidance, and a safe environment, we support every model from their first test shoot to international campaigns.

We represent faces from different cultures, identities, and backgrounds, because everyone deserves a place in front of the camera. We celebrate uniqueness. We celebrate individuality. And we are here to create the future of fashion

about the founder:

Jana Lechner

As the founder and director of this agency, I have always believed that the fashion world should inspire — not intimidate. Before creating this company, I spent years observing the industry, discovering both its beauty and its challenges. I saw incredible individuals being overlooked simply because they didn’t fit outdated standards. That is when I decided to build something different.

My goal has always been to give young talent a place where they feel seen, supported, and celebrated for who they truly are. I bring creativity, dedication, and a strong vision into everything we do — from the first casting to the biggest runway moment. I personally guide and mentor each model, making sure they receive the tools, confidence, and opportunities they need to succeed in front of the camera and beyond.

For me, this agency is more than a business — it is a community. A place where dreams grow, where individuality matters, and where every journey is respected. I am proud to continue shaping the future of fashion alongside a new generation of strong, authentic, and diverse talent.
